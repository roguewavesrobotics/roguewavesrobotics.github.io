Who we are

We are a high school robotics team that is composed of motivated and dedicated students that competes in the annual MATE ROV Competition.

Where we're from

We are from North Hollywood High School. Our team is run entirely by students, and we are source our funding ourselves through contribution from family, friends, local businesses, and community leaders.

Competition

This year, we are shooting high, and intend to join teams from around the world at the International MATE ROV Competition, at Long Beach Community College.

We utilize our high school course knowledge into our robotics program; learning their applications in the real world through competition.
